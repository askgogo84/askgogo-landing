Questions

Everything you
want to know.

Do I need to download an app?
+
No. AskGogo lives inside Telegram, which you likely already have. Open the bot link, click Start, and talk naturally. Nothing to install, nothing to learn.
How is this different from ChatGPT?
+
Three things: AskGogo uses Claude AI (smarter reasoning, better at following instructions), it has persistent memory across all conversations, and it actively reminds you — it's not just a chatbot, it's an assistant that takes action on your behalf.
Is my data private?
+
Yes, absolutely. Your memories and conversations are stored securely in your own encrypted database. We never sell data, never show you ads, and never share your information with third parties. You can delete everything anytime.
Does it really work with voice notes?
+
Yes. Record any voice message in Telegram and AskGogo transcribes it using Whisper AI (OpenAI's speech-to-text) and processes it exactly as if you typed it. Works flawlessly in English, perfect while driving or walking.
When will WhatsApp be supported?
+
WhatsApp is coming soon. We launched on Telegram first because it's faster, more reliable, and has a better API for AI assistants. Once we cross 100 paying users, WhatsApp support will be added at no extra cost for all existing subscribers.
Can I cancel anytime?
+
Yes. No contracts, no lock-ins. Cancel your subscription from your dashboard anytime and you'll stay on the paid plan until the end of your billing cycle, then automatically move to the Free plan with all your memories intact.
