Questions

Everything you need to know

Do I need to download an app?
+
No. AskGogo works inside Telegram, which you likely already have. Just open the bot and start talking. Nothing to install.
How is AskGogo different from other AI assistants?
+
Three things: it uses Claude AI (smarter than ChatGPT), it has a persistent memory that grows over time, and its reminders are precise to the minute — not approximate. It's built to actually remember your life, not just chat.
Is my data private?
+
Yes. Your memories and conversations are stored securely in your own database. We don't sell your data, show you ads, or share anything with third parties.
Can I use voice notes?
+
Yes. Send any voice message — AskGogo transcribes it using Whisper AI and processes it exactly as if you typed it. Works great while driving or walking.
Will WhatsApp be supported?
+
WhatsApp is coming soon. We launched on Telegram first because it's faster and more reliable for an AI assistant. WhatsApp support will be added for all existing users at no extra cost.
What happens when I hit my daily limit?
+
AskGogo will let you know and show you upgrade options. Your memories and reminders continue working — only new AI conversations are paused until midnight IST when the counter resets.
